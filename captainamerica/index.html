Captain America

Profile

Super soldier and founding member of the Avengers with exceptional leadership skills and combat experience.

Website: www.captainamerica.com

Experience

Avengers

Founding member and leader of the Avengers, a team of superheroes dedicated to protecting the world from threats.

Has led the Avengers on numerous missions, including the defeat of Thanos and Loki.

United States Army

Served in the United States Army during World War II, participating in numerous battles and missions.

Was awarded the Medal of Honor for his actions in the Battle of the Bulge.

  • Superhuman strength, speed, and durability

  • Immunity to most diseases and toxins
  • Enhanced senses
  • Regenerative healing factor
